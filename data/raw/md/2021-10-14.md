## 2 QUOTES FROM OTHERS

I.

Writer and essayist **Lu Xun** on the power of collective hope:

“Hope is like a road in the country; there was never a road, but when many people walk on it, the road comes into existence.”

*Source: The Epigrams of Lusin (found in*[*The Wisdom of China and India*](https://archive.org/details/wisdomofchinaind00liny)*)*

---

​II.

Politician and Noble Peace Prize winner **Aung San Suu Kyi** on corruption:

“It is not power that corrupts but fear. Fear of losing power corrupts those who wield it and fear of the scourge of power corrupts those who are subject to it.”

*Source:*[*From her speech, “Freedom from Fear”*](https://awpc.cattcenter.iastate.edu/2017/03/21/freedom-from-fear-1990/)
